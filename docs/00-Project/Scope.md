# Project Scope

## In-Scope
- Lab and workshop registration and capacity monitoring.
- Equipment and consumables inventory tracking.
- User authentication and role-based authorization.
- Multi-level booking approval workflows.
- Interactive scheduling calendars.
- Asset maintenance tracking, damage reporting, and work orders.
- QR-code scanning for check-in/out and asset details.
- Lab safety compliance declarations and incident reporting.

## Out-of-Scope
- Integration with external bank gateways (no financial transactions within SPMP).
- Automatic physical locking of laboratory doors (RFID logging only).
